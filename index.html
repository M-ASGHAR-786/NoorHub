📖 The Sacred Guide to NoorHub

1. Sacred Recitation Counter (Dashboard)

Tapping the central circular dial increments your recitation count. Click the rows on the left **Daily Dhikr Ledger** to change focus and automatically load the Arabic script, translation, and virtue. Resetting the session resets only the active circle back to 0 without deleting your recorded daily totals.

2. Prophetic Routine Matrix (Daily Practices)

Acts are laid out chronologically. Tap complete to log an act; this updates your daily progress percentage. Reversing out of detail pages preserves your filtered chronological category.

3. Spiritual Planner & Dynamic Calendar

Calendar day tiles color-grade automatically. You can edit Today and Yesterday's logs. Any days before Yesterday are locked for editing but remain completely clickable to view past logs and analytics.

4. Interactive Qaza Adjustments & Fasts

Toggle **Adjust Debts** inside your planner to unlock numerical input field adjusters for direct bulk modifications (+ or -) to missed prayer obligations.

5. Streak Consistency Rule

Complete all daily obligatory prayers on time (Qaza makeups do not count) and at least 6 Prophetic Acts to maintain your streak.

📊 Spiritual Telemetry

Prophetic Acts Tracking

Measures your daily commitment to voluntary routine practices of the Prophet ﷺ. Logging at least 6 out of the 15 chronological acts is required each day to satisfy your basic baseline.

Habit Streak Rules

Unbroken days of consistency. To preserve your streak, you must offer all 5 daily obligatory prayers on time (Qaza makeups do not count) **and** complete at least 6 Prophetic Acts before the day ends.
